## Top 3 Detailed Recommendations

### 1. BAAI
- **Thesis:** BAAI’s advanced multilingual embedding model (bge-m3) with semiconductor-specific terminology accuracy and hybrid search capabilities positions it well to capture AI semiconductor market demand.
- **Strengths:**
  - Large token capacity (8192 tokens) supports complex queries.
  - Technical moat through specialized semiconductor terminology embedding.
  - Strong team with domain expertise and solid execution capability.
- **Key Risks:**
  - Implementation complexity due to hybrid search architecture may delay product maturity.
  - Unclear monetization strategy and limited commercial traction.
  - Exposure to competitive and regulatory risks.
- **Action:** Prioritize due diligence on commercial adoption, revenue model, and risk mitigation plans.
